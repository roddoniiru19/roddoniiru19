About Me

I am a Computer Systems Technology student at Saskatchewan Polytechnic in Saskatoon, SK. Along with full-stack development in React and mobile development in Android, I also have experience from coursework using Java, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and C# to build interactive applications, data-processing utilities, and web projects.

Before starting CST, I completed my Automotive Service Technician program and worked in fast-paced environments at Ens Toyota and DSI Underground. That experience taught me how to troubleshoot problems methodically, communicate clearly with customers, and stay calm under pressure, skills I now apply to software development and technical support.

In my recent projects, I have worked with React, TypeScript, FastAPI, Python, Node.js, SQLite, and Android (Kotlin/Java). I enjoy building reliable, user-friendly tools, such as a public art mapping app, an internal grant application system, and a TaskTracker Android app for anyone who wants to stay organized.

Career Goals

My short-term goal is to begin my career in an IT support or junior developer role where I can combine hands-on troubleshooting with software development. Long-term, I want to specialize in building tools that make technical workflows easier, whether that’s for internal teams or public-facing applications.
